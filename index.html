It is a game played between you and computer, who make moves in turns until one of them cannot make any move. The player who cannot make a move loses the game and the other player is declared the winner. The game is played with several pile of coins. Each turn player can take out nonzero coins from one of the pile, or add already taken coins to one of the pile. Given the initial state of game, with several pile of coins, already taken out coins by players and you have to win the game. You move first.
